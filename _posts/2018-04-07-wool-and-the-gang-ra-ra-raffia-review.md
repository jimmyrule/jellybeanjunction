Ra-Ra Raffia is the latest addition to the amazing yarn created by [Wool And The Gang](https://www.woolandthegang.com/), its made from 100% long wood fibre and comes in 4 beautiful natural shades.

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_4743-768x768.jpeg">

I’ve never crocheted with raffia, it’s always been something that I felt was more suited to wrapping presents so I was very intrigued to give it a go! There are currently 8 crochet kits available, ranging from simple plant hangers to summer bags and storage baskets, all with a very summery, middle eastern vibe.

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_4761-768x768.jpg">

The kit I chose to try out was the ‘Money Honey Clutch Bag’, a simple design which will make a great addition to a summer outfit (if the summer ever arrives in the UK of course!). It’s the first time I’ve worked from one of WATG’s kits and I have to say how impressed I was with everything about it! Beautifully packaged in their unmistakable style it included everything you needed to make the bag – 1 cone of Ra-Ra Raffia, money honey clutch bag crochet pattern, 3.5mm hook, darning needle and a magnetic fastening. I think it would make a perfect travelling project as everything fits neatly into the bag it comes in!

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_5082-768x768.jpg">

The bag was very simple to make, perfect for beginners and the raffia itself was surprisingly easy to work with. It has a suggested hook size of 4.5mm however the pattern requires a 3.5mm hook which gave the bag a more solid texture. The pattern was well written and easy to follow, the booklet also included stitch information and simple diagrams to help if required.

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_5159-768x768.jpg">

I’m really pleased with my finished [Money Honey Clutch ](https://www.woolandthegang.com/product/money-honey-clutch-bag/crochet-your-own)and think I will get a lot of use out of it, I loved making the bag and found the kit a pleasure to work with. Wool And The Gang products are a little more expensive than other brands but from what I have experienced you certainly get what you pay for.
