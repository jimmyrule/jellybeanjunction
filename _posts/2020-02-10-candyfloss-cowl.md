Once I had decided on a project I asked Rico to send me a couple more balls of the Essentials Super Chunky and the Fashion Light Luxury, I had a cowl design that would be super simple and use both yarns together. I have included the pattern below if you’d like to make your own.

## **Candyfloss Cowl Pattern**

### You will need:

- 2 x balls of Rico Essentials Super Chunky (Shade 004 – Lilac)
- 2 x balls of Rico Fashion Light Luxury (Shade 022 – Neon Fuchsia)
- 9mm crochet hook

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_7273-2048x2048.jpeg">

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/FFC1CBBB-D125-4C46-9348-CAB7AD62DDCA-2048x2048.jpg">

### Terms:

- ch – Chain
- tr – Treble crochet (US Double Crochet)
- ss – Slip Stitch

### Pattern:

To make the cowl you will be holding both yarns together at the same time. From row 2 onwards you will be working your stitches in the space between stitches rather than in the top of them.

Holding both yarns together loosely chain 25.

Row 1 – tr into the 4th ch from the hook, tr along to the end, turn. (23 trs)

Row 2 – 2ch, tr across (in between the stitches) to end, turn.

Row 3 to 33 – Repeat row 2, fasten off and break yarn (leaving a long tail to finish the cowl).

Holding wrong sides together whip stitch together through back loops only, this creates a lovely neat join.
