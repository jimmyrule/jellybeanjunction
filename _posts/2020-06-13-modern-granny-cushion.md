I decided to use my favourite colours (pink and yellow) and my favourite stitch pattern (granny squares) to create a pretty cushion, the free pattern is below.

Happy Hooking 🙂

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_8543-1024x1024.jpeg">

You will need:

- Whims Merino (worsted weight) Pink x 4 balls **(colour A)**
- Whims Merino (worsted weight) Mustard x 2 balls ***(colour B)***
- 4.5mm (size G+) crochet hook
- 35cm x 35cm cushion pad
- Stitch markers or pins to help join the cushion panels.

Terms:

- tr – Treble Crochet (US Double Crochet)
- ch – Chain
- st – Stitch
- sl st – Slip Stitch
- sp – Space
- mc – Magic Circle

Notes:

- Pattern written in UK terms.
- Tie off ends when changing colours, crochet over ends where possible to reduce number of ends to sew in.
- Beginning chain ALWAYS counts as a stitch.

Pattern:

*Square 1 (Make 8)*

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_9030-768x768.jpeg">

1. With **A** make a mc, (working into mc) 3ch, 2tr, 2ch, 3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 3tr, 2ch, 3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, ss to join.
2. 3ch, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, 2tr), ss to join.
3. 3ch, 2tr, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, 3tr, (3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, 3tr, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, 3tr, (3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, ss to join, fasten off and sew in ends.

*Square 2 (Make 4)*

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_9032-1-768x768.jpeg">

1. With **A** make a mc, (working into mc) 3ch, 2tr, 2ch, 3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 3tr, 2ch, 3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, ss to join.
2. 3ch, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, 2tr), ss to join.
3. 3ch, 2tr, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, 3tr, (3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, 3tr, **change to A**, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, **change to B**, 3tr, (3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, ss to join, fasten off and sew in ends.

*Square 3 (Make 4)*

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_9031-1-768x768.jpeg">

1. With **B** make a mc, (working into mc) 3ch, 2tr, 2ch, 3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, 3tr, 2ch, 3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, ss to join.
2. 3ch, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, (3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 2tr), ss to join.
3. 3ch, 2tr, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, 3tr, (3tr, 1ch, **change to A**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, 3tr, **change to B**, (3tr, 2ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, **change to A**, 3tr, (3tr, 1ch, **change to B**, 1ch, 3tr) in 2ch-sp, ss to join, fasten off and sew in ends.

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/Whims-cushion-chart.jpg">

Once you have finished all the squares – arrange them as per the layout chart, sew together using whip stitch in the back loops and **yarn** **A**.

When all the squares are joined add an extra round of 3tr clusters using **yarn A**. Join yarn with a ss in any 2ch-sp and work around the edge, ss to join and fasten off.

*Cushion Back*

Using **yarn A**, crochet an 8 round granny square.

Construction:

Hold the panels together, wrong sides facing. Use stitch markers or pins to line the corners up.

Using **yarn A**, whip stitch around the first 3 sides, insert the cushion pad and then sew up the last side to finish.
