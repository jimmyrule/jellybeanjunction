I have recently been given the chance to review a fabulous new crochet book which is soon to be published – something I have never done before so was very excited to be asked!

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_0185-2-768x768.jpeg">

‘Home Decoration in Crochet’ is written by the very talented crochet designer Tanya Eberhardt, you may have come across her blog [Little Things Blogged](http://www.littlethingsblogged.com/) – a great source of inspiration for crochet and knitting. The book is published by [Tuva Publishing](https://www.tuvapublishing.com/) who specialise in craft books which means it is clearly laid out, full of colour and a joy to read.

October is a busy time of year for us crafters as we start making lots of things for Christmas. As my spare time was limited I wanted to make something relatively quick from the book which contains 25 colourful projects. I have a big stash of aran cotton yarn which I thought would work well for several of the smaller designs.

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_9882-1-768x768.jpeg">

The project I decided to make was the ‘Elegant Purse’ which is a small purse/handbag with a simple but really effective design and a very easy to follow pattern. Using Drops Paris yarn I made the main part of the purse in grey and decided to use a contrasting colour for the side panels.

The simple stitches used to make the purse create such an amazing texture, I found it to be a really relaxing project and it only took me a few evenings to complete.

<img class="rounded img-fluid post-img mx-auto d-block" src="/assets/images/IMG_0234.HEIC_-1-768x768.jpeg">

I absolutely love this book, its got a fantastic variety of projects and would suit beginners and more experienced crocheters. I highly recommend getting your hands on a copy when you can.

Happy hooking x
